## Acknowledgment Policy

- Acknowledgments are provided **at SSR’s sole discretion**.
- Only good-faith, coordinated disclosures that follow our Security Policy are eligible.
- We currently offer **no monetary rewards or bug bounty program**.
- Being acknowledged does **not** imply endorsement, employment, or any ongoing relationship with SSR.
- Researchers may request credit under a real name, handle/pseudonym, or remain anonymous.

To request acknowledgment, include your preferred credit name/handle in your initial report or follow-up communication to security@sargentum.systems.
