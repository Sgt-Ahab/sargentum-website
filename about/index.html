Mission Statement

Sargentum Systems & Research is a private entity focused on cutting edge theories, and the execution of those theories. Making our services available to the public is the root of our mission, bringing the discoveries forward with strict principles to allow for free market usage.

Meet Dave!

Dave is the founder of Sargentum Systems & Research, an aspiring Computer Scientist gathering his academic and professional experience to pave the way for SSR to become the American standard of business operation, with full intention of investing heavily into domestic American families. Once SSR is fully operational, Dave has plans for forming Academies for the youth and Research Bounties within the company to spur development. Dave is a strong believer in the founding principles of the country that saved his ancestors from fascist abuse, and is determined to make the country a better place for the next generation of Americans.
